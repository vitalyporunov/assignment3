About This Quiz App

Welcome to our interactive quiz app, designed to make learning fun and engaging! Test your knowledge with a variety of questions on different topics, track your progress, and challenge yourself with customizable time limits.

Features

  • Multiple choice questions
  • Interactive feedback with correct answers
  • Progress bar to track your quiz journey
  • Timer to add a challenge
  • Customizable themes to personalize your experience
  • Social media sharing to compare scores with friends

Accessibility

We are committed to making our quiz app accessible to everyone. We have incorporated accessibility features such as semantic HTML. alternative text for images, keyboard navigation, and sufficient color contrast.

Feedback

We welcome your feedback! If you have any suggestions for improvement. find any issues, or have ideas for new features, please don't hesitate to contact us.
